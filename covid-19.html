5th April 2021

As promised, we are now able to announce that The Sheps will reopen on Tuesday 4th May.

As long as Boris’s roadmap out of lockdown stays to plan, we will be able to welcome you indoors from Monday 17th May, with this in mind, please note that going indoors until this time, will only be possible to access the toilets.

We hope the British weather is kind and you can enjoy The Sheps new “Secret Garden”, a space we have reclaimed back from nature during lockdown.

If you wish to book a table for your group (up to 6 people) please give us a call on 01823 339602

It has been a long time and I speak for all the team when I say “We cannot wait to see you all soon”. You lovely people make “The Sheps” what it is and without you here it’s been a very sad time indeed.

Here’s to the future and the return of “Good Food, Good Ale & Good Times”

Take care of yourselves, be healthy and be happy.
